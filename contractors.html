What I Build for Each Client

  • Google Business Profile
    Full GBP optimization, verification, suspension recovery, photo management, and review response automation. Managed via custom Playwright browser automation where Google's API doesn't reach.
  • Google Search Console
    Property verification, sitemap submission, indexing monitoring, and search performance tracking across all 184 accounts.
  • Google Ads Campaigns
    Portfolio-wide campaign setup and management via the Google Ads API. Automated campaign creation at day 31 of each client's lifecycle with pre-flight checks and ad copy generation.
  • GoHighLevel CRM
    Workflows, pipelines, tag automations, missed-call text-back, review request sequences, nurture campaigns, and cold outreach. Every client gets a fully automated sales pipeline from lead to close.
  • A2P 10DLC Compliance
    Migrated 800+ sub-accounts through A2P registration, including the emergency migration from GHL to SignalHouse when the agency's A2P access was shut down.
  • Browser Automation
    Custom Playwright scripts that automate GBP Manager, Google Search Console, and GHL at scale. Handling operations that have no API, across hundreds of accounts simultaneously.
  • AI Voice Agents
    Voice AI routing and deployment for inbound call handling, Q&A extraction, and automated call summaries. Connected to GHL workflows for seamless follow-up.
