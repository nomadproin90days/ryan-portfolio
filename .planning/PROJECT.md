# LuxeTide Lead Engine

## What This Is

A cold email outreach engine that drives inbound discovery calls to Ryan Christmas via luxetidestudio.com. Modeled after the AI Architechs playbook (cold outbound via GHL to US business owners), but inverted: instead of placing cheap offshore talent, this positions one premium US-based AI automation specialist (Ryan) for project-based engagements ($3-10K+). The site already exists and converts — this project builds the pipeline that feeds it.

## Core Value

Business owners who need AI automation find Ryan and book a discovery call — consistently, without Ryan manually prospecting.
